# Customer Offer Research Example

Prompt:

```text
Use $structured-root-cause-research to analyze why my AI/security service offer is not converting and propose a 7-day validation plan.
```

Expected style:

```markdown
## Problem
The offer is technically strong but too broad for a buyer to understand quickly.

## Evidence
| Evidence | Source | Confidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Offer mixes AI, security, full-stack, and automation | offer copy | high |
| Buyer has no concrete deliverable, timeline, or price anchor | offer copy | high |
| Similar services sell clearer outcomes: audit, report, PoC, automation workflow | market examples | medium |

## Root Cause Summary
Why 1: Prospects do not know what they are buying.
Why 2: The offer describes capabilities instead of a painful business outcome.
Why 3: There is no narrow package with deliverables, price range, and proof.
Root cause: The service needs packaging before lead generation can work.
Confidence: high

## Recommendation
Package one narrow service, create a sample report, score 30 leads, and send 10 personalized messages per day for one week.
```
